Rolling Rivals

Game Description

The game is designed for two players to compete against each other, and the objective is to reach 100 points or more to win the match.

Game Rules :

  • 1. Player 1 initiates the game and takes the first turn by rolling the dice. If Player 1 rolls any number between 1 and 6 (excluding 1), the corresponding score is added to their total score, and they earn another chance to roll the dice.
  • 2. When a player rolls a 1 or decides to hold the cumulative score, the active player switches to the other player. In such a situation, the turn passes to the opponent, allowing them to take their turn by rolling the dice and accumulating points accordingly.
  • 3. This alternating gameplay continues until one of the players reaches or exceeds 100 points, and they are declared the winner of the match.
